Welcome to "lovelf"~

Lovelf web app is dedicated to supporting individuals facing mental health challenges. There are tools and resources available in this web app to encourage them to take control of their mental well being.

1. TreeHole (Journaling)
2. Self-care practices
3. Helpline

We hope to help individuals navigate through their mental health journey with confidence and resilience!
